What We Do

First Gear is your gateway drug into car culture. We are the first stop for those who do not know a lot about cars but want to be gently introduced to the latest news, hottest trends, and routine maintenance. We create approachable content that your grandma can understand. Our goal is that next time you visit the mechanic you will be confident and not feel like you have been taken advantage of or better yet, you can fix it yourself! So come along, we are glad you came.
